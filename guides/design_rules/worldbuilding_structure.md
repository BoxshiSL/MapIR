# Worldbuilding structure

Curated from "The busy writer's guide to worldbuilding." and "Worldbuilding
Guide for Video Games." in `guides/`.

## Foundations

* **worldbuilding_arena_boundaries** — define where the world starts and
  ends, the dominant conflict, and the factions in tension *before*
  decorating.
* **district_identity_differentiator** — districts express conflict and
  values, not just visual style.

## What weak worldbuilding looks like

Players ask:
* who are these factions?
* why are they in conflict?
* why are there invisible walls?
* who are the bad guys?

Strong worldbuilding answers those *implicitly* via geography, district
identity, and visible scene slots.

## Builder mindset

Structural integrity first; shiny details later. v0.5's deterministic
generation pipeline mirrors this — block out parcels before placing
buildings, place landmarks before fussing with materials.
