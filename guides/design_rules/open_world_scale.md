# Open-world scale-illusion principles

Curated from "Как сделать открытый мир игры больше, чем он есть на самом
деле." in `guides/`.

## Key rules

* **scale_illusion_hide_some** — partial visibility supports curiosity;
  filling the map with markers from minute one kills exploration.
* **readability_label_clutter** — too many overlapping markers turn the
  preview (and the in-game minimap) into noise.

## Other notes

* Forests, fog, terrain, darkness, framing — all are tools for controlling
  visibility and apparent scale.
* Use distant goals and partial visibility together.
* Important: hiding information must create curiosity, not frustration.
* Distant landmarks + small neighbouring objects imply scale.
